I am a Systems and Reliability Engineer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I build production systems that bring AI, integrations, and infrastructure into business workflows.

My background is in full-stack engineering, and today I build the systems that make AI useful in production. I design integrations, document intelligence pipelines, knowledge graphs, retrieval, orchestration, and evaluation that connect models to real business operations. The goal is simple: build software that is reliable enough for people to trust, not just impressive enough to demo.

## Areas of Focus

- **Orchestration & State Machines:** Designing deterministic execution flows around probabilistic model behaviors using explicit state machines and event logs.
- **Evaluation & Diagnostics:** Authoring robust evaluation benchmarks (e.g. Harbor and Terminal Bench style tasks) and factual grounding pipelines (using NLI-based verification like MiniCheck).
- **Backend & Event-Driven Systems:** Architecting highly available systems, telemedicine platforms, headless ERPs, and local/international payment integrations.
